About Us

A local company built on trust, reliability, and straightforward service

D & M Plumbing and Heating is positioned as a family-owned Pennsylvania business that values honest work, strong communication, and long-term customer relationships.

Our Story

Serving local homes with practical solutions and dependable workmanship

D & M Plumbing and Heating was built around a simple idea: local homeowners deserve a service company that shows up when it says it will, communicates clearly, and stands behind its work. That mindset has helped shape a business focused on responsive service, respectful technicians, and repairs that solve the real issue instead of masking symptoms.

As a family-owned local company, the goal is not just to complete one service call. It is to become the first name customers think of when they need help with plumbing repairs, heating system issues, water heaters, drain cleaning, or urgent leak problems.
